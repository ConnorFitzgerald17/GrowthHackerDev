# Editorial QA Checklist (v1)

1. Verify title is clear, specific, and aligned with target keyword/theme.
2. Verify excerpt accurately summarizes the article in 1-2 sentences.
3. Verify factual claims have source validation or internal proof.
4. Verify code snippets run or compile in stated environment.
5. Verify links are valid and not broken.
6. Verify publish date and slug are correct.
7. Verify hero image and OG image are appropriate and load.
8. Verify article includes a newsletter CTA and relevant next step.
9. Verify required attribution is visible when `brandingRequired=true`.
10. Final pass for grammar, readability, and developer relevance.
